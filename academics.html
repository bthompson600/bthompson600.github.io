Academics

St. Benedict Preparatory School

In June of 2024, I graduated from St. Benedict Preparatory School in Chicago, IL. St. Benedict was a private preschool through 8th grade school that offered many classes, electives, and clubs. I had to work a lot academically to graduate and receive my diploma. I graduated with all A's and as one of the highest ranking students of my class.


Lane Tech

For my highschool of choice, I chose Lane Tech. Lane Tech is a public highschool in Chicago that offers many classes, clubs, sports, and a diverse community. My older brother, who is now a junior in college, went to Lane Tech. This provided many opinions and first-hand experiences to go off of. I am now about 2/3 of the way through my Freshman year, and it has been great.
